## The brief

The fraud function needed a system that could do more than flag suspicious users. It needed to unify device telemetry, reviewer workflows, machine-assisted scoring, and enforcement logic into one operating layer.

## What changed

- Built repeatable Databricks and Delta pipelines that joined telemetry with outcomes data.
- Created user-level risk features that could be reused by policy, enforcement, and review operations.
- Introduced a unified risk score that reduced manual review pressure without sacrificing control over false positives.

## Why it mattered

This work moved fraud operations away from one-off checks and toward a measurable system. The result was better prioritization, cleaner reviewer workflows, and clearer communication between analytics, Trust & Safety, Product, and Compliance.
