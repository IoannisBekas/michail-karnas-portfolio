## The brief

Tesco Till needed a measurement framework that went beyond usage counts. Leadership needed to see adoption, feature impact, and rollout quality in a way that engineering and product teams could act on.

## What changed

- Defined OKRs and performance measures for product adoption and operational rollout.
- Built data models that made customer journeys and system friction visible to engineering teams.
- Delivered deep-dive analysis that turned behavior data into prioritization decisions.

## Why it mattered

The analytics function became part of the product operating model, not just a reporting service. It gave the roadmap a stronger evidence base and contributed to major annual efficiency gains.
