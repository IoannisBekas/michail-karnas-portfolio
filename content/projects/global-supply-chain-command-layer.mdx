## The brief

Supply chain leaders across multiple markets were working from inconsistent definitions and duplicated reporting. The challenge was to create a shared analytics layer that supported planning, operations, and stakeholder alignment.

## What changed

- Standardized KPI definitions across five markets.
- Rolled out high-visibility Tableau and Power BI dashboards for operational leaders.
- Improved planning support for seasonal cycles, promotions, and fulfillment decisions.

## Why it mattered

The analytics estate became easier to trust and easier to operate. Teams had a common language for performance and a simpler path from reporting to action.
